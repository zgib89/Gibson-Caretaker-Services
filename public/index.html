Good to know

Questions families often ask.

Can I arrange and pay for this from out of state?+
Yes, and many people do. You text or call me, I send you the price and the after-photos. Your mom or dad never has to handle scheduling or money — you stay in the loop from wherever you are.
How do I pay you?+
However’s easiest for you, like a check. I never take gift cards or wire transfers, and I never ask for payment before you’ve agreed to the price.
Do I need to be home? Do you keep a key?+
Whatever’s comfortable for you — you don’t need to be home for outdoor work. I never keep a key any longer than the visit itself.
Which towns do you cover?+
Darlington and the towns right around it. Not sure if I reach you? Just give me a call — if I can’t help, I’ll try to point you to someone who can.
How do I get started?+
Just call or text (608) 341‑7826. No apps, no accounts, no forms — a real conversation with a real neighbor.
